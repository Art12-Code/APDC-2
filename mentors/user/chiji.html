Dr. Chiji Ojukwu

Managing Director, Africa Projects Development Centre
Mentor

Dr. Chiji Ojukwu holds a BSc in Agricultural Economics from the University of Ife, now Obafemi
Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Nigeria, and an MSc and PhD in Development Economics from the
University of Bradford, UK.

Whatsapp Facebook Contacts

What can i do?

He is an experienced director with a demonstrated history of working in the development industry, skilled in policy analysis, strategic planning, international development, economics, program management, and youth empowerment.
Until recently, He served as the Director of Agriculture and Agro-industry at the African Development Bank (AfDB), holding various positions during his 21-year tenure, including Regional Director of Southern Africa B, and Manager of the North, East, and South Region. He also functioned as a Task Manager for a decade. Before joining the African Development Bank, Dr. Chiji Ojukwu was the National Project Coordinator at the then Federal Agricultural Coordinating Unit, a World Bank-assisted Federal Government of Nigeria project overseeing donor-funded agricultural projects in Nigeria. He has extensively taught and written on project analysis and applying Cost-Benefit Analysis to Development Projects.
Since 2018, he has been a senior consultant with Agriculture & Food Security at the Brazil Africa Institute, providing advisory and program development services. His unwavering dedication to empowering young people and creating opportunities for their growth and development led to the establishment of the APDC Group, a youth-led organization. At the APDC Group, his vision for a more inclusive and equitable society resonates deeply with all of us. The young talents here are motivated to pursue excellence and positively influence the lives of others through his work ethic and passion. It is admirable how committed he is to giving young people and women opportunities for growth and development and to empowering them.
